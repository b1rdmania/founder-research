# Founder Research Prompt

Use verbatim with `perplexity_research`. Substitute [FOUNDER FULL NAME] and [COMPANY NAME].

---

Deep research on [FOUNDER FULL NAME], founder of [COMPANY NAME].

I need:

1. **Background**: nationality, education, career before this business — name roles and companies
2. **Origin story**: how and why they started the business, in their own words if available. Direct quotes preferred.
3. **Communication register**: how do they speak publicly? Warm or cold, first-person or corporate, expert or accessible, educational or promotional
4. **What they care about**: values, stated mission, causes they support, what they say drives them
5. **Public output**: named interviews, podcasts, press features, conference appearances — with dates and outlets
6. **Social presence**: LinkedIn and other active accounts — what they post about, how often, what tone. Note if they're mostly absent.

Prioritise direct quotes over paraphrasing. Flag anything that reveals what they find energising versus what seems draining or delegated. If the founder has low public profile, say so clearly.

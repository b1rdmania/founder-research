# Pricing Research Prompt

Use with `perplexity_search` or `perplexity_research`. Substitute [COMPANY NAME], [COUNTRY], and [COMPETITOR LIST].

---

Find retail prices for [COMPANY NAME] products in [COUNTRY].

Also find retail prices for these direct competitors: [COMPETITOR 1], [COMPETITOR 2], [COMPETITOR 3].

Present as a table:

| Brand | Product | Price | Unit | Channel |
|---|---|---|---|---|

Note:
- If prices vary by retailer, list the range
- If prices are not publicly listed, say so — **price opacity is itself a finding**
- Include the channel where each price was found (e.g. brand website, Ocado, Amazon, Planet Organic)
- Note any recent price changes if visible

Do not estimate. Report only confirmed prices with their source.

# Market Research Prompt

Use verbatim with `perplexity_research`. Substitute [VERTICAL] and [COUNTRY/REGION].

---

Deep research on the [VERTICAL] market in [COUNTRY/REGION].

I need:

1. **Market size**: current value with source and year, plus 3-5 year projections and CAGR
2. **Top competitors**: 4-6 named competitors with — positioning (one line), pricing (specific numbers), distribution channels, one notable recent move each
3. **Category trends**: what is mainstreaming, what is contracting, what is emerging — with evidence not opinion
4. **Distribution dynamics**: which channels are growing (e.g. DTC, specialty retail, supermarket), which are being squeezed, where new entrants are winning
5. **Customer profile**: who buys, how they discover the category, what drives switching between brands

Be specific. Name companies, cite figures with sources and dates. No filler. If a trend is widely discussed but not supported by data, say so.

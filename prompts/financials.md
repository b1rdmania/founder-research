# Companies House Financials Prompt

UK businesses only. Use with `perplexity_research`. Substitute [COMPANY NAME] and [COMPANY NUMBER] if known.

---

Find the latest Companies House filing for [COMPANY NAME] (company number [COMPANY NUMBER] if known).

I need:

- **Balance sheet** for the last 2 filed years: total assets, total liabilities, net equity/shareholders' funds
- **Cash at bank** — specifically, not just current assets
- **Number of employees** — as filed
- **Registered charges**: any bank loans, debentures, or security interests registered against the company
- **Director history**: full appointment and resignation log with exact dates for all directors ever listed

If the accounts are micro, abridged, or dormant — say so and give what's available. Note the filing date so I know how recent the data is. If accounts are overdue, flag that.

Do not estimate or infer figures. Report only what is in the filing.

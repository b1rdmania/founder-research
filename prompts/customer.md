# Customer Signal Prompt

Use with `perplexity_search`. Substitute [COMPANY NAME].

---

Find customer reviews, ratings, and community discussion about [COMPANY NAME] and their products.

Check these sources specifically:
- Trustpilot
- Google reviews
- Amazon product listings
- Reddit (any subreddit)
- Facebook groups
- Retailer listing pages (e.g. Ocado, Planet Organic, Not On The High Street)
- Food or product blogger coverage
- Instagram comments if accessible

I need:
- Overall sentiment (positive / mixed / negative)
- Recurring praise — exact phrases customers use
- Recurring complaints — exact phrases customers use
- Any influencer or press mentions that include customer response
- Total review count and date of most recent review

**If there is no review presence after several years of trading, say so clearly — that is itself a finding.** Do not fill the gap with generalities.

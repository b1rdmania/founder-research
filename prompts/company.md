# Company Research Prompt

Use verbatim with `perplexity_research`. Substitute [COMPANY NAME] and [DESCRIPTION].

---

Deep research on [COMPANY NAME] — [one-line description, e.g. "a UK-based kimchi and fermented food manufacturer"].

I need:

1. **Company basics**: founding date, founders, legal entity name, registered address, Companies House number if UK
2. **Products and pricing**: full product range with retail prices if publicly available
3. **Distribution**: where products are sold — name every retailer, platform, wholesale account, or export market mentioned
4. **Brand positioning**: how they describe themselves, who their stated target customer is, their key marketing claims
5. **Recent news**: funding rounds, partnerships, awards, expansions, controversies, leadership changes — last 3 years only
6. **Online presence**: website quality and apparent investment, social channels with follower counts, content cadence (how often they post)

Named facts with sources only. Do not pad with industry generalities. If you can't find something, say so rather than inferring.
